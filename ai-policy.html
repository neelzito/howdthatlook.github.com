AI Content Policy

Understanding AI-Generated Content in how'dthatlook

Last updated: December 19th, 2024

Important: AI-Generated Content

All images created or modified by this app use artificial intelligence. Generated content should be clearly identified as AI-created when shared and should not be presented as authentic photographs.

1. What is AI-Generated Content?

HOWDTHATLOOK uses advanced artificial intelligence models to create and modify images. This means:

  • Artificial Creation: All output images are generated or modified by computer algorithms, not captured by cameras
  • Based on Training Data: AI models are trained on large datasets of images to learn patterns and styles
  • Interpretation, Not Reality: Generated content represents an AI's interpretation of your request, not factual reality
  • Clearly Labeled: All AI-generated content in the app is marked with ✨ AI-Generated labels

2. How Our AI Works

Understanding our AI features helps you use them responsibly:

Professional Headshots

  • Transforms your photo into professional-style portrait
  • Changes lighting, background, and styling
  • Maintains facial features while enhancing presentation

Hairstyle & Outfit Changes

  • Digitally applies different hairstyles or clothing
  • Blends new elements with your original photo
  • Results are artistic interpretations, not exact previews

Photo Restoration

  • AI reconstructs missing or damaged parts of old photos
  • Enhances quality and removes artifacts
  • Restoration is an educated guess, not historical accuracy

Background & Object Changes

  • Removes objects or changes backgrounds intelligently
  • Fills in missing areas using AI prediction
  • Creates seamless but artificial results

3. Responsible Use Guidelines

✅ Appropriate Uses

  • Personal experimentation with different looks
  • Creative projects and artistic exploration
  • Visualizing potential style changes
  • Enhancing old family photos for personal use
  • Creating fun, clearly-labeled AI content for social media

❌ Prohibited Uses

  • Creating fake identification documents
  • Generating misleading representations of real people without consent
  • Creating content intended to deceive or manipulate others
  • Producing inappropriate, illegal, or harmful content
  • Processing images of people under 18 years old
  • Violating others' privacy or likeness rights

4. Understanding AI Limitations

AI-generated content has inherent limitations:

  • Accuracy: Results may not accurately represent how you would actually look
  • Bias: AI models may reflect biases present in their training data
  • Inconsistency: Similar inputs may produce different results
  • Quality Variation: Output quality depends on input image quality and complexity
  • Cultural Context: AI may not understand cultural or personal significance

5. Sharing AI-Generated Content

When sharing content created with our app:

  • Always Disclose: Clearly label content as AI-generated
  • Use Hashtags: Include #AIGenerated or #ArtificialIntelligence tags
  • Add Context: Explain that it's a digital creation, not a real photograph
  • Respect Platforms: Follow each social media platform's AI content policies
  • Consider Impact: Think about how others might interpret your shared content

6. Privacy and AI Processing

Your privacy is protected during AI processing:

  • Temporary Processing: Images are only temporarily sent to AI services for processing
  • No Storage: AI service providers do not permanently store your images
  • No Training Use: Your images are not used to train or improve AI models
  • Local Storage: Generated images are stored only on your device
  • Secure Transmission: All data is encrypted during transmission

7. Content Moderation

We implement safeguards to prevent misuse:

  • Content Filtering: AI services include filters to prevent inappropriate content
  • Usage Monitoring: We monitor for patterns of misuse
  • Reporting System: Users can report concerning generated content
  • Model Updates: We regularly update AI models to improve safety

8. Age and Consent Requirements

Important Age Restrictions:

  • Do not upload images of anyone under 18 years old
  • Obtain explicit consent before processing others' photos
  • Parents should supervise children's use of AI features
  • Consider the ethical implications of generating content of real people

9. Commercial Use

For commercial or professional use of AI-generated content:

  • Licensing Required: Commercial use may require additional licensing
  • Legal Compliance: Ensure compliance with applicable advertising and media laws
  • Client Disclosure: Always disclose AI generation to clients or audiences
  • Professional Standards: Follow industry standards for AI-generated content

10. Reporting Misuse

If you encounter inappropriate AI-generated content or have concerns about misuse:

  • Contact Us: Report concerns to our support team immediately
  • Provide Details: Include specific information about the concerning content
  • Platform Reporting: Also report to relevant social media platforms
  • Legal Issues: Contact appropriate authorities for legal violations

11. Policy Updates

This AI Content Policy may be updated as technology and regulations evolve. We will notify users of significant changes and update the "Last updated" date above.
