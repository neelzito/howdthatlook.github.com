Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11th, 2025

At Yay, a product of Killbridge Ventures Pte Ltd, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our service, including our website and mobile applications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide to us: We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload photos, or contact customer support.
  • Information we collect automatically: When you use our website or mobile applications, we automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our service.
  • Mobile device information: When you use our mobile applications, we may collect additional information such as the type of mobile device you use, your device's unique identifier, and your mobile operating system.

1A. Face Data

While our app allows users to upload and process photos that may contain faces for AI-based restyling, we do not collect, store, or process biometric data or face identification data. The images are processed for visual transformation only, and no facial recognition or biometric analysis is performed.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our service
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices and updates
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Develop new products and services

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information about you as follows:

  • With third-party service providers: We may share information with vendors who help us provide our service.
  • For legal reasons: We may share information if we believe it's necessary to comply with applicable laws or legal processes.
  • Business transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will also retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. Your Choices

You can update, correct, or delete your information by accessing your account settings. You may also contact us directly to request access to your personal information.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the bottom of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Killbridge Ventures Pte Ltd.

9. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Singapore. Any disputes arising under this Privacy Policy will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Singapore.
