Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18th, 2025

At MILIVON, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how HOWDTHATLOOK ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. Our app is designed with privacy-first principles - no user accounts required, local image storage, and anonymous usage only.

1. Privacy-First Design

HOWDTHATLOOK is built with privacy as a core principle:

  • No user accounts: You can use the app without creating an account or providing personal information
  • Local storage only: Your photos and generated images are stored only on your device
  • Anonymous usage: We use a randomly generated identifier (UUID) stored securely in your device's keychain
  • No cloud storage: Your images are never permanently stored on our servers or third-party servers

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our AI image generation service:

  • Device identifier: A randomly generated UUID stored securely in your iOS keychain for anonymous usage tracking
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous data about app features used and generation requests (no personal information)
  • Purchase data: In-app purchase transactions handled securely by Apple's StoreKit (we only receive confirmation of successful purchases)
  • Technical data: App version, iOS version, and crash reports to improve app stability

3. Image Processing and AI Services

When you generate images using our AI features:

  • Temporary processing: Your images are temporarily sent to Replicate's AI service for processing only
  • No permanent storage: Images are automatically deleted from Replicate's servers after processing (typically within minutes)
  • No facial recognition: While images may contain faces, we do not perform facial recognition, biometric analysis, or identity verification
  • Processing only: Images are used solely for the AI transformation you requested (headshots, hairstyles, etc.)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

  • Replicate: AI image generation service that temporarily processes your images
  • Supabase: Optional anonymous usage analytics and credit management
  • Apple StoreKit: Secure in-app purchase processing

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. Your images are only sent to these services when you actively request AI generation.

5. Credit System and Purchases

Our credit-based system works as follows:

  • Credit tracking: Your credit balance is stored locally on your device
  • Purchase verification: We verify purchases through Apple's secure systems
  • Anonymous transactions: Purchases are linked only to your anonymous device identifier
  • No financial data: We do not collect or store payment information (handled by Apple)

6. Data Retention and Storage

  • Local data: Images and app data remain on your device until you delete them
  • Server data: No personal images are permanently stored on any servers
  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage data may be retained for up to 2 years for app improvement
  • Purchase records: Maintained by Apple according to their retention policies

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete app data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Reset identifier: Reinstalling generates a new anonymous identifier
  • Control image sharing: Images are only processed when you explicitly request AI generation
  • Purchase history: Managed through your Apple ID account settings

8. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure storage: Device identifier stored in iOS keychain with hardware encryption
  • Encrypted transmission: All data sent to AI services uses HTTPS encryption
  • Minimal data: We collect only what's necessary for app functionality
  • No account vulnerabilities: No user accounts means no account-based security risks

9. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification since no personal data is collected.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Company: MILIVON
  • Email: privacy@milivon.com
  • Website: www.milivon.com

12. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of your jurisdiction. For users in the European Union, this policy complies with GDPR requirements for anonymous data processing.
